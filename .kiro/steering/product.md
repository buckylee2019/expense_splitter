# Product Overview

SplitX is an expense-sharing application similar to Splitwise, designed for splitting expenses among friends and groups.

## Core Features

- User authentication and profile management with avatar support
- Group creation and management with multiple members
- Expense tracking with flexible split configurations (equal, percentage, exact amounts)
- Multiple payer support for complex expense scenarios
- Settlement tracking and balance calculations
- Category-based expense organization
- Reporting and analytics with charts
- Multi-group settlement optimization

## Key Concepts

- **Groups**: Collections of users who share expenses together
- **Expenses**: Individual transactions with configurable splits among group members
- **Settlements**: Records of payments made to settle balances between users
- **Balances**: Calculated debts and credits between users within groups
- **Categories**: Expense classification for better organization and reporting

## User Experience

- Mobile-responsive design with dedicated mobile detection
- Photo upload support for user avatars (stored in S3)
- Real-time balance calculations
- Pagination for large datasets
- Visual reports with pie charts for expense categories
